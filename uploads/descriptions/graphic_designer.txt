About Red BJJ Apparel:

Red BJJ Apparel is a leading brand in the Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu community, renowned for our commitment to quality, innovation, and the BJJ lifestyle. We create high-performance apparel that meets the specific needs of BJJ practitioners, from beginners to world-class athletes. Our mission is to support the BJJ journey through products that inspire and innovate. As we expand our product line and reach within the global BJJ community, we are looking for a talented Graphic Designer to join our team.

Position Summary:

The Graphic Designer will play a crucial role in developing and executing creative concepts for Red BJJ Apparel's product designs, marketing materials, and digital content. This position requires a blend of creativity, technical proficiency, and a deep understanding of BJJ culture to produce designs that resonate with our audience. You will work closely with our product development and marketing teams to bring new ideas to life and maintain our brand's position at the forefront of BJJ apparel.

Key Responsibilities:

Design and produce engaging graphics that reflect our brand's identity for a variety of uses, including apparel, accessories, marketing materials, social media content, and website design.
Collaborate with the product development team to conceptualize and create innovative designs for new product lines.
Work with the marketing team to develop and implement creative solutions for campaigns, promotions, and online content that drive brand engagement and sales.
Manage multiple projects from concept through completion under tight deadlines.
Stay up-to-date with trends in design, technology, and BJJ culture to ensure our brand remains relevant and appealing.
Provide quality control over concepts and projects to ensure consistency and integrity of the brand.
