Role Overview:

As a Backend Developer Intern, you will play a crucial role in our development team, contributing to the architecture, development, and maintenance of our backend systems. You will work closely with our experienced engineers to enhance your coding skills, understand system design, and participate in the lifecycle of software development from concept to deployment.

Responsibilities:

Assist in the development and maintenance of backend services, ensuring high performance and responsiveness to requests from the front-end.
Contribute to the creation of scalable, secure, and efficient code.
Collaborate with the frontend team to integrate user-facing elements with server-side logic.
Participate in code reviews to maintain code quality and encourage best practices.
Support in the identification and resolution of bottlenecks and bugs.
Engage in the ongoing learning of backend technologies and coding standards.

Currently pursuing or recently graduated with a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
Proficiency in backend programming languages such as Python, Java, Node.js, or Ruby.
Familiarity with database technologies (e.g., MySQL, MongoDB), web servers (e.g., Apache, Nginx), and UI/UX design.
Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.
A passion for technology and an eagerness to learn about new tools and technologies.
